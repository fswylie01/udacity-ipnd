Chimpanzee

Chimpanzees are one of our planet’s ‘great apes’, along with gorillas and orangutans.

  • Family Name: Hominidae
  • Average Height: 1-1.7m standing
  • Average Lifespan: 45 years
  • Habitat: Tropical forests

They have long arms that extend below their knees, short legs and black hair covering almost their entire body. Their faces are flat with big eyes, a small nose and a wide mouth.
